  This comprehensive guide helps parents understand the differences between IEPs

  what to expect during meetings, and how to effectively advocate for your
  child's educational needs.


  ## What's Included:


  - **Understanding the Basics**: Clear explanations of IEPs vs 504 Plans

  - **The Evaluation Process**: Step-by-step guide to requesting and understanding evaluations

  - **Meeting Preparation**: How to prepare for IEP/504 meetings

  - **Your Rights**: Understanding IDEA and Section 504

  - **Advocacy Tips**: Effective strategies for advocating for your child

  - **Sample Documents**: Templates and examples

  - **Resources**: Additional reading and support organizations


  ## Who This Guide Is For:


  - Parents of children with autism

  - Caregivers navigating special education

  - Educators seeking to better support families

  - Anyone involved in the IEP/504 process


  This 45-page guide is written in clear, accessible language and includes real-world examples, checklists, and actionable advice.
